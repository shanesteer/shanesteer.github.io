WHAT IS THE PROJECT?

This is my first ever experience using Unity to develope a simple game. This game was developed in the space of a week for my Challenge Week in Second Year at university. I learnt alot about Unity and its features during this week. The aim of this Racing Game is to try and get the best possible lap time while racing against simple AI opponents.

FEATURES

  • Acceleration, reversing and braking
  • Basic AI opponents to race against

DEVELOPMENT

Multiple programs and websites were used throughout the development of Super Speed. The programs & websites used are:

  • Unity - Game Engine
  • OBS - To record videos for demos and trailers
  • Google - To get 2D car images and race track
  • Assetstore.unity.com - To get game assets and animations
